Common Questions
- Do bundles rollover? Depends on network; MTN/Airtel typically allow rollover within validity.
- Can I gift data? Yes, most networks support gifting; charges apply.

Troubleshooting Connectivity
- Check data balance via USSD.
- Toggle airplane mode or reboot device.
- Ensure mobile data is enabled and APN is correct.
- Test SIM in another device to isolate device issues.
