Supported DISCOs
- EKEDC, IKEDC, AEDC, PHEDC, IBEDC, EEDC, KEDCO, BEDC, JEDC, KAEDCO

Common Questions
- How long to receive token? Usually instant; allow up to 5 minutes.
- What meter types are supported? PREPAID (tokens); POSTPAID (bill payments) varies.

Token Loading Steps (General)
1) Enter the 20‑digit token on your meter keypad
2) Press the Enter/Confirm key
3) Wait for success tone or display confirmation

Troubleshooting
- “Rejected” or “Old Credit Used”
  - Ensure correct meter number; try re‑entering token.
  - If still failing, contact DISCO support with reference.
- “Process Failed”
  - Power cycle the meter and retry. If persistent, contact your DISCO.

Safety
- Keep tokens and receipts secure. Never share meter tokens publicly.
