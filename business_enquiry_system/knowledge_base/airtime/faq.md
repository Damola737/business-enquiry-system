Common Questions
- What networks are supported? MTN, Airtel, Glo, 9Mobile.
- Delivery time? Usually instant; allow up to 5 minutes if network is busy.
- Minimum/Maximum amounts? ₦50 – ₦50,000 per transaction.

Troubleshooting
- I didn’t receive airtime but my card was charged
  - Wait 5 minutes and check balance.
  - If still missing, verify recipient number and share reference with support.
- Wrong number entered
  - Transactions are final; contact support with details to review options.

USSD Balance Codes
- MTN: *310# (balance), *312# (data)
- Airtel: *310# (balance), *323# (data)
- Glo: *310# (balance), *312# (data)
- 9Mobile: *310# (balance), *323# (data)

Security
- Never share your full card PAN/CVV on chat.
- Use only official self‑service links for payments.
