Title: Airtime Pricing & Limits (NGN)
Version: 1.0
Updated: 2025-11-04
Domain: AIRTIME
Tags: pricing, limits, discounts, faq

- Minimum purchase: ₦50 per transaction
- Maximum purchase: ₦50,000 per transaction
- Bulk discount: 5% off for single purchases ≥ ₦10,000
- Fees: No platform fee for airtime purchases

Notes
- Some banks or card issuers may add their own fees.
- Discounts apply automatically at checkout for eligible amounts.

Examples
- ₦1,000 purchase → pay ₦1,000
- ₦15,000 purchase → 5% discount (₦750) → pay ₦14,250
